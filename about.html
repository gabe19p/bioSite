Madison Purselley is from the heart of East Texas but has now ventured across the world with her husband. Top destinations for her would be Paris, Sydney and Rome. As a military spouse, she has learned to adjust to a less traditional way of life. She’s always wanted to help people and went to school to work in the dental industry, but as military life moves, so does she. Her current adventure has her embracing the Japanese lifestyle on the tropical island of Okinawa. She assists special needs children with their learning journey. When she isn’t at work, she enjoys spending her free time cooking or creating content. She has recently taken up the sport of golf with her husband, while they document their journey together with vlogs. Her passion for YouTube has helped her grow as a storyteller and content creator. She plans to enjoy her time in Japan and then move back to the United States to start her family and career at a dental office.
